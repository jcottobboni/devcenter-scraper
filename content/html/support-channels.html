Heroku provides a number of support channels.

Status

Check out Heroku Status to find out if the Heroku platform is experiencing any issues. Learn more about Heroku Status.

Dev Center

Visit Dev Center to find documentation on everything from the Heroku basics to advanced Heroku features, as well as general application development best practices.

Developer community

Engage in our Developer Community by seeking or providing advice on application and development level questions. We have a very strong community of developers using our platform and you can help make it stronger.

Heroku Help

Seek help by querying all of the above resources from a single location at Heroku Help, allowing you to quickly gain answers to your Heroku specific questions and open a ticket directly with our support staff. You can open a support ticket directly with our support staff if you are unable to find an answer to your question. We will respond to your ticket within a few business days.
